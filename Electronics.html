Dhairya: Electronics Projects

  1. Design and optimization of Power Generation model for nano-satellites.

    (Jan 2021 - Feb 2021) Team Anant Topics: Power electronics, MPPT, Solar Cells and Battery
    • Optimized and implemented the MPPT algorithm in Simulink using a boost converter as a controller with more than 99% tracking efficiency.
    • Optimized boost converter from 60% to 81% efficiency by altering inductor and diodes.

  2. Design and optimization of Power Distribution model for nano-satellites.

    (Feb 2021 - May 2021) Team Anant Topics: Power electronics, Batteries, Circuit Design
    • Designed and optimized buck converters(5V and 3.3V) in Simulink.
    • Implemented RC filters for the reduction in voltage spikes due to frequent switching of components.
    • Modeled an Overcurrent protection circuit to protect components from overcurrent situations.

  3. Design of Over-current protection circuit with Auto Restart.

    (Non 2020 - May 2021) Team Anant
    • Designed and simulated an Overcurrent protection Circuit for low current(around 2mA) threshold for MSP430 microcontroller.
    • Implemented Auto restart functionality using an RC circuit.
